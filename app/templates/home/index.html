Access to the World

With an estimated 2.46 billion users worldwide, social media provides an unprecedented opportunity to gauge global consumer sentiment. It is our ambition to harness this untapped data and extract insight into the cryptocurrency market.

Volatile Markets

The cryptocurrency world is volatile and unregulated. Frequently coins experience significant percentage swings caused by breaking news or rumours that gather momentum across the social media network.
